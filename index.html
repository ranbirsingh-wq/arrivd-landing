How Arrivd works
for you

From syncing your inbox to tracking every delivery — here's the experience, step by step.

1

Sync your email

Connect the email you shop with. Arrivd scans your inbox and pulls in every order confirmation automatically.

2

See every order

All your purchases in one place — filter by status, group by retailer, and never lose track of a shipment again.

3

Track deliveries live

Real-time tracking across carriers. Know exactly where your package is and when it'll arrive at your door.

4

Returns & insights

Start returns with one tap. See spending breakdowns by category, merchant, and time period — take control of your habits.
